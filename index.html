Macros, short for macronutrients, are the three key nutrients that make up the majority of the food we eat: protein, fat, and carbohydrates. To find the macros in your food, check the nutritional information on the packaging or look up the food online. You can also use a food tracking app to get the macros for specific foods. Be sure to measure the portion size of your food to get an accurate count of the macros you are consuming.

What to do on this page:

  • Enter your daily intake of fat, carbs, and protein in grams.
  • Click the "Calculate" button to see your total calorie intake.
  • Use the results to plan your meals and track your progress.
