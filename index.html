Tracking and Calculating Calories

Welcome to myCaloriepal! Here's how you can use our website to track and calculate your calories:

  • To get started, use the "Calculate BMI" tab to calculate how many calories you need to consume per day based on your goals (gain weight/maintain/lose weight).
  • Next, use our built-in API to track what you consume per day. This will give you the macronutrients and calories each food has.
  • Finally, use the "Track Progress" page to track the total consumption of calories per day and monitor your progress towards your goals.
